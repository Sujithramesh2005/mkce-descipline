Discipline Rules

  • Students must maintain a clean shave.
  • Formal shoes are mandatory.
  • Formal dress code must be followed.
  • Identity card must be worn at all times inside campus.
  • Mobile phones are not allowed during class hours.
Note: Adherence to the above rules ensures a professional and respectful campus environment.

If any of the above rules are not followed, please submit the compliance form below.
